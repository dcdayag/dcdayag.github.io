What is the website all about?

The Eagle's Eye, the Eye for the society. This website is created for our english project proposal. This website contains five images in total and a satirical essay about a global hurdle that should be solved as soon as possible. This social issue is Poverty. This website's objectives is to educate the readers about this well-known society issue and to let this issue spread for more awareness on how this global issue affects us and everything around us. Poverty is a sensitive topic in the society, especially in my country, the Philippines. Here, people living in poverty has been exponentially increasing especially during the pandemic we are curretly facing. Thousands of people lost their jobs. As more people are living in poverty, the higher the chances that the economic growth would remain negative. We need to stop and cease this issue as soon as possible. But, how? Let's start by helping one another.

Introducing Poverty

Poverty, one of the biggest social problems of the world, has been gradually spreading throughout the Philippines. One of the reasons why poverty is still an issue is because of the continuous decline of the economy our country is facing. Moreover, the pandemic the world is facing had a huge role regarding the economy's declining phase. Economic conflicts and natural disasters, like Climate Change, are the causes of environmental poverty. Furthermore, these issues lead to hunger and malnutrition. As the levels of population growth are increasing, the GDP per capita is decreasing. Also, high levels of population growth cause a lack of employment and education as people living in poverty cannot afford to support the increasing number of members of their families. In conclusion, poverty is caused by; economic decline, corruption in business and politics, and high levels of population growth.
