About the Author & Website

Hello! I'm Drei Cerise B. Dayag. This website isn't just only for my CS project, but also to relay awareness about the biggest issue in the Philippines which is Poverty. The eagle eye is among the strongest in the animal kingdom, with an eyesight estimated at 4 to 8 times stronger than of the average human. This website is the Eagle of the People and is the eye of the society. The main objective in the creation of this webpage is to educate the readers about this well-known society issue and to let this issue spread for more awareness on how this global issue affects the reader and everything around us. My motivation to being the Eye for the people is to see the happy smile of those people living in poverty.
